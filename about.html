About Us

Humble beginnings

Peeva Restaurant is a small catering service provider formerly known as "Eva Adalia Catering Service". In the year 1990, the owner, Eva Adalia started to offer packed lunch to her officemates. Eventually, Eva was able to attract a market where she could offer her products. She then decided to put up her own catering service and as years passed, she was able to expand her business by putting up Peeva Restaurant, a place where people dine comfortably at an affordable cost. It also boasts an elegant event's place where people can celebrate every occasion with great food and service.

Mission

Our mission is to satisfy our customers by delivering food service with CLASS; to provide QUALITY service by offering the customers a wide variety of food choices while keeping the AFFORDABILITY of the prices.

Vision

Peeva Restaurant envisions itself to become one of the largest food providers that will offer quality food and excellent service.
